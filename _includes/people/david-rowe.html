David Rowe

Senior Manager, City Engagements

Member of

  • Staff Alumni

David joined Code for America in July 2014. Most recently, he served as the Director of Policy in Kansas City, Missouri, Mayor Sly James's office, where he tracked, managed, and implemented the Mayor’s public policy agenda. His work ranged from tracking the citywide business plan and budget, to staffing KCStat (a data-driven performance management initiative), to acting as a liaison to Google Fiber’s implementation team in Kansas City. David’s role also included working with the Mayor’s Challenge Cabinet to implement the city’s open data policy, and he was the primary point of contact for Kansas City’s 2013 Code for America Fellowship. He enjoys life and BBQ in Kansas City and is an avid baseball fan. David holds a bachelor’s degree from Wake Forest University and a law degree from the University of Kansas.
